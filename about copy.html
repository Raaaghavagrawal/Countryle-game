Thanks For Visiting!

Welcome to Country Wordle, an exciting and educational variation of the classic Wordle game. In this version, instead of guessing random words, your challenge is to identify the names of countries from around the world. The idea for this game came to me after seeing a flag-guessing game on a repository by Shivasnh Pratap Singh, paired with my familiarity with the Wordle concept. This inspired me to create a unique game where players guess country names. I was also fortunate to receive assistance from my friend during development, and you can explore his social media as well. Special thanks to Akshat Omar for his invaluable guidance throughout the process.
